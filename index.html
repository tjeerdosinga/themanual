The Flux-Beam-Thingie-Device

How to unbox your Flux-Beam-Thingie-Device

Easiest way to unbox your Flux-Beam-Thingie-Device is to follow below steps carefully

  • Place the box on a stable surface
  • Open the box very carefully as temporal flux is highly unstable
  • Take out the fluxbase
  • Take out the beambase
  • Combine the fluxbase and the beambase so it becomes the Flux-Beam-Thingie-Device.
  • Uncombine the fluxbase and the beambase as you need to install them seperately
Wash your device before first use

Only use the best soap and wash by hand

soap wash

Never use laundry machines or dishwashers as still will disturb the temporal flux

local_laundry_service dishwasher_gen
Install the fluxbase

Connect the fluxbase to your MSX keyboard device with a large parralel printer cable. Any newer cables might disturb the temporal flux. Turn on your MSX and switch on the flux base. Use following code to active first stage of temporal flux stabilisation

.fluxActivation (install flux balancer) .fluxStabilization (compile flux balancer) .fluxInitial (start flux balancer) .fluxBase (confirm that all your bases belong to us) .fluxBase {override and run programm}

Disconnect the fluxbase and put in fridge to cool down

Install the beambase

Connect the beambase to your MSX keyboard device with a large parralel printer cable. Any newer cables might disturb the temporal flux. Turn on your MSX and switch on the flux base. Use following code to active first stage of temporal flux stabilisation

.beamActivation (install beam balancer) .beamStabilization (compile beam balancer) .beamInitial (start beam balancer) .beamBase (confirm that all your bases belong to us) .beamBase {override and run programm}

Disconnect the fluxbase and put in fridge to cool down

Code the base programm

take both the beambase and the fluxbase out of the fridge and combine both device. Connect again with large parralel printer cable. Use following code to finalise the base programm

.beamBlueTooth (activate blauw tand) .beamWiFi (activate wij fij) .beam5G (activate vijf geee) .beamBase (confirm that all your bases belong to us) .beamBase {override and run programm}

Disconnect cable and press the ON-Button. Stage 1 is ready

Code the flux programm

Connect parralel printer cable to flux base. Use following code to finalise the flux programm

.fluxBlueTooth (activate blauw tand) .fluxWiFi (activate wij fij) .flux5G (activate vijf geee) .fluxBase (confirm that all your bases belong to us) .fluxBase {override and run programm}

Disconnect cable and press the ON-Button. Stage 2 is ready

Verify your code in IOS or Android

Download the Flux-Beam-Thingie-Device from the either the Appstore or Google Play. Open the application and click on the terminal icon

terminal

Enter following code to link your Flux-Beam_Thingie-Device with your app

.fluxConnect_search_blauw_tand_or_wij_fij_or_vijf_gee .fluxConnect_automated_connection_override_just_confirm .fluxGreen_switch_on_all_elements .fluxConnect_confirmed_ready
Set Where-to Time Jump

Exit the terminal and select the TimeJump icon

send_time_extension

Enter following code to link your Flux-Beam_Thingie-Device with your app. Change dd.mm.yyyy to your own likings

.setTimeJump{date.dd.mm.yyyy} .confirmTimeJump
Establish your return time pull

Exit the TimeJump icon and select the TimeReturn icon

energy_program_time_used

Enter following code to link your Flux-Beam_Thingie-Device with your app. Change minutes to your own likings. Be aware that this device can only handle time travel for a maximum 59 minuten at a time

.setTimeReturn{minutes} .confirmTimeReturn
Final Steps on your Flux-Beam-Thingie-Device

make sure to sit on the ground. Time Travel can be very disorienting. When you are ready to TimeJump, press the now flashing blue botton on your device. Enjoy!
